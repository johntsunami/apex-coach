WHAT APEX COACH DOES FOR YOU
ADAPTIVE TRAINING
Your workouts adapt daily based on how you feel — sleep, energy, stress, and soreness all shape your plan. No two sessions are the same.
INJURY-SMART PROGRAMMING
Report any condition or injury and the app automatically filters out unsafe exercises and adds rehabilitation protocols. Built from 100+ clinical condition protocols reviewed by licensed physical therapists.
EVIDENCE-BASED PERIODIZATION
Your training follows the Optimum Performance Training model — 5 phases from stabilization through power. You'll progress through each phase as your body is ready.
FULL BODY ROM MODULE
A complete head-to-toe range of motion system covering every joint in every plane — exactly like a physical therapy session.
CARDIO FITNESS TRACKING
VO2 max testing, heart rate zone training, and 38+ cardio exercises across every gym machine and outdoor activity.
BODYBUILDING & PHYSIQUE
Evidence-based hypertrophy programming with advanced techniques — drop sets, rest-pause, supersets, and periodized competition prep.
POWER & BODYWEIGHT MASTERY
Plyometrics, explosive training, and progressive bodyweight challenges from wall push-ups to one-arm push-ups.
PT REHABILITATION
Mini-sessions with before/after pain tracking, daily reminders, and criteria-based progression for every condition.
AI COACHING
Two modes: Health Coach for exercise science questions and Mental Health Coach for CBT, DBT, and motivational support.
YOUR JOURNEY DASHBOARD
See your complete long-term plan, 6-week cycle goals, milestone badges, and progress across every training module.
SAVE APEX COACH TO YOUR HOME SCREEN
For the best experience, save the app to your phone's home screen:
iPhone (Safari):
1. Open Safari and go to: apex-coach-five.vercel.app
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow) at the bottom
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" — the app icon appears on your home screen
Android (Chrome):
1. Open Chrome and go to: apex-coach-five.vercel.app
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
4. Tap "Add" — the app icon appears on your home screen
